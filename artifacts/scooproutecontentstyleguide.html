Artifact Story

Situation

A growing documentation program needed consistent standards that could support multiple writers, product teams, content types, and user roles. Basic grammar rules were not enough to address mobile workflows, stressful customer situations, release changes, accessibility, or AI assisted drafting.

Artifact Story

Task

I needed to create a reusable content system that helped contributors make consistent decisions about voice, terminology, procedures, interface language, errors, warnings, visuals, and customer communication.

Artifact Story

Action

I defined the product voice and its tonal variations, documented major user personas, established terminology and interface label rules, and created guidance for procedures, errors, empty states, release notes, accessibility, screenshots, mobile content, customer messages, and AI governance.

Artifact Story

Result

The guide provides a shared editorial foundation across product content. It makes documentation easier to write and review, reduces inconsistent terminology, improves usability, and establishes clear expectations for responsible AI assisted work.

This sample was recreated from general professional practices without proprietary standards or internal documentation. It shows the voice principles, persona cards, two writing-pattern examples, and the AI-assisted writing checklist — the full standards stay private.
