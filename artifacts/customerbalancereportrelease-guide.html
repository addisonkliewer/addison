Artifact Story

Situation

The previous report made it difficult to understand how individual deliveries, wallet payments, credits, refunds, and event deposits contributed to a customer's total balance. Teams often had to combine multiple exports or inspect several product screens.

Artifact Story

Task

I needed to explain what changed, why the previous workflow was insufficient, how the new controls worked, and what each affected team should do before adopting the report.

Artifact Story

Action

I used before and after framing to establish the workflow change. I documented the new grouping, date, status, credit, and column controls; connected each option to a practical use case; identified affected roles; and included preparation and training guidance.

Artifact Story

Result

The article translates a dense reporting update into recognizable billing and operational decisions. It helps teams configure useful report views, create richer exports, reduce manual reconciliation, and explain balances more clearly to customers.

This sample uses fictional business logic and contains no customer financial data. It shows the problem statement, a condensed before-and-after comparison, and the suggested saved report views — not the complete article.
