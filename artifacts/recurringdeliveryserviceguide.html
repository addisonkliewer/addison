Artifact Story

Situation

Creating a recurring delivery service required users to coordinate customer information, delivery cadence, route availability, truck capacity, service areas, and notification settings. Without structured guidance, users could publish an incomplete service or discover scheduling conflicts too late.

Artifact Story

Task

I needed to create a guide that helped users configure and publish recurring service while understanding the operational consequences of each decision.

Artifact Story

Action

I organized the guide around the order in which the work happens. I introduced the workflow, explained available delivery cadences, documented route and service area settings, surfaced inventory and scheduling warnings, and clarified what happens after the service is published.

Artifact Story

Result

The finished guide gives users a predictable path from initial setup through publication. It also connects the customer workflow to future route planning, driver instructions, and customer communication.

This sample is original fictional content created for public portfolio use. It shows the setup overview, the route availability check, and the post-publication summary — the complete procedure isn't reproduced here.
