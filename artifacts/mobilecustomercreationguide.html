Artifact Story

Situation

Field employees needed to create customer records from either a map location or a traditional customer list. The workflow included permissions, address validation, service areas, notifications, delivery preferences, additional contacts, and optional billing details.

Artifact Story

Task

I needed to document both entry points while helping users understand which information was required, how location data should be reviewed, and when the customer record officially existed.

Artifact Story

Action

I separated the map and customer tab workflows, then consolidated their shared fields into one structured reference. I added realistic examples, validation reminders, permission guidance, and repeated the critical save behavior where users were most likely to miss it.

Artifact Story

Result

The guide gives mobile users a clear path through a potentially long form while reducing incomplete records, incorrect addresses, and assumptions that an unsaved customer can already be scheduled.

This sample was created without proprietary workflows, internal documentation, or customer data. It shows both workflow entry points, one customer detail section, and the save reminder — not the complete guide.
