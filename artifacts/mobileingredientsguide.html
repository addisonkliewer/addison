Artifact Story

Situation

Drivers needed to record ingredient usage while working from trucks, events, and delivery stops. The workflow included ingredient quantities, units, lot numbers, expiration dates, allergen labels, dietary labels, storage states, and permission restrictions.

Artifact Story

Task

I needed to make a detailed data entry workflow usable on a mobile device without minimizing the safety and historical record implications of the information.

Artifact Story

Action

I combined concise navigation steps with structured field definitions, realistic examples, permission guidance, search behavior, warning language, and troubleshooting. I also explained why historical ingredient records could not simply be deleted.

Artifact Story

Result

The guide supports fast mobile data entry while giving office teams more reliable records for inventory analysis, customer questions, allergen review, and food safety investigation.

This sample contains no proprietary product details or customer data. It shows the field definition pattern, the allergen warning, and the missing-ingredient troubleshooting checklist — not the complete guide.
