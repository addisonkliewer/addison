Pause a Customer's Deliveries

A fictional help article showing how I document temporary account state changes, customer communication, and downstream operational impact.

Artifact Brief

Key context for this sample

This sample focuses on a deceptively sensitive workflow. Pausing service affects the customer account, future routes, driver visibility, and customer expectations, so the article must clearly distinguish a temporary pause from permanent cancellation.

Artifact Story

Situation

Customers sometimes needed to temporarily stop deliveries because of vacations, seasonal closures, billing issues, or service changes. The workflow affected active routes, notifications, and the customer's future service schedule.

Artifact Story

Task

I needed to explain how to pause and resume service while preventing users from accidentally canceling the customer or leaving outdated deliveries on a published route.

Artifact Story

Action

I documented prerequisites, pause reasons, start and end dates, internal notes, customer messages, driver impact, resumption steps, and common troubleshooting scenarios. I also explained when cancellation was the more appropriate action.

Artifact Story

Result

The guide makes the state change visible to every affected audience. Support teams can explain what happened, dispatchers can update routes, and customers receive a clear expectation about when service will resume.

This sample is adapted for public portfolio use with fictional product details. It shows the pause reason fields, a sample customer message, and one troubleshooting scenario — not the complete article.
